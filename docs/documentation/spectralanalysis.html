function simdiag{T <: DenseOperator}(Ops; atol, rtol)¶

Simultaneously diagonalize commuting Hermitian operators.

This is done by diagonalizing the sum of the operators. The eigenvalues are computed by \(a = \langle \psi |A|\psi\rangle\) and it is checked whether the eigenvectors fulfill the equation \(A|\psi\rangle = a|\psi\rangle\).

Parameters:Ops – Vector of operators (sparse or dense).
Keyword atol (optional):
 kwarg of Base.isapprox specifying the tolerance of the approximate check Default is 1e-14.
Keyword rtol (optional):
 kwarg of Base.isapprox specifying the tolerance of the approximate check Default is 1e-14.
Returns:
  • evals_sorted – Vector containing all vectors of the eigenvalues sorted by the eigenvalues of the first operator.
  • v – Common eigenvectors.
